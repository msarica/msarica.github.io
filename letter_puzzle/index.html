Puzzle Generator

How to Play

  • Type a message in the box above.
  • Add a hint to help solvers
  • Click Generate Puzzle to create your ciphered puzzle.
  • Each letter is replaced by a unique number. Spaces and punctuation stay the same.
  • Kids can use the letter map at the bottom to match numbers to letters as they solve!
  • Print it!
